What to Look for in a Good Propane Smoker

Before you go through the many, many, propane smokers the industry has to offer, it’s important to know exactly what you’re looking for. Here are some questions to ask yourself while selecting a propane smoker:

  • How easy is it to set up?
  • What’s the quality of the build?
  • How dependable is it? Does it last?
  • What maintenance is involved?
  • Is using the smoker straightforward or complicated?
  • Is the burner proportionate to the gas smoker’s cabinet?
  • Is the burner protected from exposure?
  • What kind of fuel does it use?
  • How much does it cost?

Overall, you’re looking for a smoker that you can set up with no problem with well-constructed parts and performs consistently for an even, thorough cook. Finding a smoker that needs the least amount of maintenance is a win because a difficult clean can deter you from using the smoker often. Finally, the price of the smoker should be within reason with the features it offers.

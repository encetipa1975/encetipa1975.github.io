I was looking forward to reporting tomorrow that Wargame: Red Dragon, the third and final entry in Eugen’s Wargame series of real-time tactical modern war games was going to be available to grab for free, via the Epic Games Store.

For those of you who don’t know, since nearly the beginning Epic have been giving away free games every week via their own digital storefront, the Epic Games Store. Provided you grab it within the window it’s being offered, it’s yours to keep for free forever - the only caveat is that you have to play it via the EGS launcher.

Along with their store exclusives, it has formed part of Epic’s ‘Go Big or Go Home’ strategy of trying to win customers in its bid to challenge Steam for dominance over PC gaming. There have been some pretty decent titles given away before, such as Civilization VI, but Red Dragon would have been the first ‘wargame’ to be offered.

Except now it’s not being offered, at least not this week like we initially thought. Late last night the company put out an announcement that the Red Dragon promotion was being “temporarily delayed”, with this week’s free giveaway switching to Dungeons 3 instead.
